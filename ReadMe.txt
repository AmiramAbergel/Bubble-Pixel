Game flow (how should it be solved by the user):

Here is an easy and entertaining game for you.
At first, you'll hear a chill sound that changes level by level.
You can either start the game or end it from the menu, which is the first scene in the game.
There are 5 levels in our game. In order to demonstrate many different situations of the game, we designed levels 3,4, and 5 as levels 10, 20, and 30, respectively.
With each level, a hidden pixel image appears. Your goal is to pop the bubbles in-game just like bubble wrap. When the bubbles pop they will sound like they are popping bubble wrap, and the image will change after that.
After the user will pop all bubbles, the next level will start.
The user can choose to watch the level 3 and level 5 advertisements including the ads to pop 50% of the bubbles..
In levels 4 and 5, we are demonstrating levels 20 and 30, we want our users to enjoy the game without working hard, and so our game will stop when 80% of bubbles are popped, and the unique pixel picture will be revealed!
We hope that you enjoy the game as we did!
